Help
Welcome to the Bonanza Mall help section. We're here to help you with any questions or concerns you may have about our website and our products. Below are some of the most common questions we receive. If you don't find the answer you're looking for, please don't hesitate to contact us via email, phone, or live chat.

Placing an Order
Follow these steps:
  1. Browse our website
  2. Sign in/Sign up Account
  3. Fill All information correctly
  4. Add the products you want to your cart
  5. Confirm payment using jazzcash or easypaisa

Shipping and Delivery
How long will it take for my order to arrive?
We guarantee delivery within 2-3 business days, no matter where you are in Pakistan.
Do you offer international shipping?
No, we currently only ship within Pakistan.
Can I receive order tracking ID?
Yes, you will receive a order tracking number once your order has shipped.

Returns and Refunds
What is your return policy?
We have a hassle-free return policy if the product is not unboxed. Simply contact us within a certain time frame to initiate the return process.
How long does it take to process a refund?
Once we receive your returned item, we will process your refund within a certain time frame.
Will I be refunded for shipping costs?
No, we do not refund shipping costs.

Account and Profile
How do I create an account on Bonanza Mall?
You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the website. Kindly fillup the form correctly. We will use your information in order delivery.
How do I update my account information?
You can update your account information by logging into your account and navigating to the "My Account" section of the website.

We hope this help section has been useful. If you have any further questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us. Our customer service representatives are available 24/7 to assist you.
